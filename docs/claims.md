# Claims

- C1: Sparse planner-exploited transition errors can dominate embodied success more than average prediction loss.
- C2: A guarded transition patch can stop repeated exploitation after a counterexample in a deterministic finite model.
- C3: CCRA is distinct from system identification, residual correction, uncertainty, or verifier-only safety.
- C4: The paper is mechanistic and diagnostic, not a claim of full real-robot SOTA.
